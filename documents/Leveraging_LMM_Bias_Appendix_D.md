**3. Grok - Surprisingly Lenient in 8-Step, Noticeably Stricter in

- Favors steelmanning more heavily than the other models

**Interpretation**

Grok behaves like an **inference-maximizing model**, willing to:

- Credit abductive plausibility

- Give borderline arguments the benefit of the doubt

- Explore alternative plausible readings\

- Analyze assumptions

- Examine soundness

- Evaluate burden-of-proof

**Interpretation**

DeepSeek functions as a **methodological maximalist**:

- Most attentive to hidden assumptions
